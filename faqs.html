FAQS

Find answers to common questions and concerns about our services and assessments.

Are assessments accepted by the NHS, education settings and employers?

Our assessments are always completed by HCPC registered, experienced clinicians. Reports follow gold standard recommended methods and meet NICE guidelines.

Do I need a referral?

You do not need a referral to access our services. Simply call us directly by phone on 0207 100 2990. Alternatively, please fill in the contact form on our website or email us at: enquiries@psychological-consultations.com

How do I make payment?

Payments are made via bank transfer and are taken at the point of booking an assessment.

Where will my face-to-face sessions take place?

Face to face sessions will take place at our clinic, 89 Fleet Street, London EC4Y 1DH. If you live outside London; you will be able to complete your assessment by completing one visit at our Fleet Street clinic and having the rest of your sessions online.

How do I attend online sessions?

You will be sent a secure link for online sessions. You will only need a computer or phone with internet access.

What happens after the assessment is completed?

You will receive a report detailing the information we gathered during the assessment process. This will also include diagnostic decisions and recommendations.
