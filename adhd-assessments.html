"We provide expert Autism and ADHD Assessments for Adults and Children

Our experts will help you to find the answers you need and suggest ways to make life easier. 

It can be difficult to know if you have ADHD or not. There are times when we all find it difficult to pay attention, act impulsively or are hyperactive. Adults with these difficulties can also mask them or learn how to work around these symptoms. What we do, is consider whether you meet criteria for ADHD and suggest recommendations even if you don’t.

All initial assessments are conducted in person at our Central London Clinic, and completed online.  

Common signs of ADHD

Difficulties waiting in queues or avoiding queues

Being impulsive when making decisions or shopping

Fidgeting frequently or feeling like you need to move around

What happens in this assessment?

Step 1

Pre-assessment information is gathered. 

Information is gathered about your current circumstances and difficulties. Information is also sent to your informant (parent, sibling or friend) to fill in.

Step 2

A clinical interview based on the ACE+

A clinical interview takes place. This covers your early history and asks about ADHD symptoms. This is completed with you, and you may also wish to bring an informant with you. The whole process takes 2 hours to complete and can be completed online.

Step 3

Feedback session, including recommendations

Lead clinician considers the evidence. If a further ADHD test called the QB test is required, this will be arranged.

Step 4

This feedback, online meeting discusses whether you now have a diagnosis and what recommendations we are giving to help.

Step 5

A report is delivered.

Holistic Evaluation and Streamlined Process

Our combined assessments offer a holistic evaluation approach, taking into account both Autism and ADHD, providing a comprehensive understanding of an individual's needs. With a streamlined process, we aim to make the assessment journey as efficient and convenient as possible.
